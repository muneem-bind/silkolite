Welcome to Pioneer Embroideries Limited

Leading Dope Dyed / Melt Dyed Polyester Yarn Manufacturer

Pioneer Embroideries Limited commenced its operation in the year 1991, as manufacturer of Embroideries, Torchon/Bobbin laces, Raschel laces and other garment accessories. Later on in 2005, decided to foray into a greenfield project of manufacturing Dope Dyed Polyester Filament Yarns and Cabled/Twisted/Air Textured Yarns/Draw Textured Yarns with state of art world renowned German technology. Within no time, we have carved a permanent niche in Dope dyed yarns all across the world with cutting edge technology and world class quality under brand

Our Products

Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY)

Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY) is produced from the melting and extrusion (melt spinning) of the polyester chip or flake.

Air Textured Yarn (ATY)

Air Textured Yarn (ATY), is a kind of hybrid textured yarn produced through Air-Jet Texturing process

Partially Oriented Yarn (POY)

Partially Oriented Yarn (POY) is produced from the melting and extrusion (melt spinning) of the polyester chip or flake.

Polyester Twisted Yarn

Twist is the spiral arrangement of the fibres around the axis of the yarn.

Draw Textursed Yarn (DTY)

Drawn Textured Yarn (DTY) is a type of polyester filament yarn produced by processing POY through a texturing process

Cabled/High Bulk Yarns

Cabling and High Bulking are processes to convert multi filament yarns suitable for applications where coarser deniers are required.

Special Products

Functional Yarn

Functional Yarn with special characteristics viz Flame Retardant, Anti Microbial, High Light Fastness and High Moisture Absorption are available

Fancy Yarn

Yarns suitable for replacing natural fibres like Cotton, Viscose and Wool for applications in Flat Knitting, Circular Knitting and Weaving
