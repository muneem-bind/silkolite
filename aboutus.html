Group Profile


Pioneer Embroideries Limited commenced its operation in the year 1991, as manufacturer of Embroideries, Torchon/Bobbin laces, Raschel laces and other garment accessories.


Later on in 2005, decided to foray into a greenfield project of manufacturing Dope Dyed Polyester Filament Yarns and Cabled/Twisted/Air Textured Yarns/Draw Textured Yarns with state of art world renowned German technology. Within no time, we have carved a permanent niche in Dope dyed yarns all across the world with cutting edge technology and world class quality under SILKOLITE brand.


We are also the proud owners of the no. 1 retail brand for embroidered clothing “Hakoba”. A clear vision along with focus on quality, creativity and innovation has resulted in this spectacular, trail-blazing success. Today, Pioneer Group has established an impeccable reputation and carved a permanent niche in the industry throughout the world. Catering to millions of satisfied customers already, Pioneer Group is now poised to move ahead in leaps and bounds.

Pioneer Embroideries Limited – “SILKOLITE” – Scale to Value


Silkolite has made a strong impression in the Indian and global polyester Dope Dyed Yarn Market since its inception in 2007.


From a humble beginning of capacities of 5000 tons per annum, we have managed to swell our capacity to 19000 tons per annum assisted by a comprehensive network across India and World. Our wide range of products, superior quality standards, professional team comprising experts on Dope Dyed Filament yarn industry and a surprisingly low Minimum Order Quantities of 500 kilos per colour sets us apart from our competitors in the polyester filament yarn market.


We have come a long way today to create a unique niche for ourselves in the Dope Dyed Filament Yarn vertical with one of the largest variety of colour offerings in yarns and being a “one stop shop” for our customers in India and abroad for their various requirements. Our main strength remains end to end tailormade solutions for customers.


Our plant is designed to produce Dope Dyed Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY) and Partially Oriented Yarn (POY) with a wide range of Deniers as fine as 30 to as coarse as 800 in all the available lustres possible. Further we have Draw Texturing machines, Air Texturing machines, Twisting machines, Cabling machines and Heat set machines (both batch and continuous) to offer highest standards of value added products and enable end to end solutions for various applications like Carpets, Warp / Weft Weaving (both apparel and non-apparel), Narrow Weaving, Knitting, Label Weaving, technical textiles like Flame retardants etc and embroidery.


Latest technology machines such as latest OerlikonBarmag technology for FDY and POY coupled with superior HR practices provide a perfect working environment for our growth. Setting new standards of quality through continuous improvement is a way of life at PEL.

At PEL we believe that “Quality is not an Act, it is a Habit”.


The major focus is on R&D on Inter Fibre replacements mainly targeting Natural Fibres and replacement of Solution Dyed Yarns.


Every kilogram of yarn consumes 35 litres of precious water to produce solution dyed yarns and our dope dyed process is totally without water and generates zero effluents making it a very ECO FRIENDLY method. So in effect a saving of 1.9 Million litres of water every day or 4.2 Million barrels per annum which is equivalent to almost 260 full size Olympic swimming pools.


All the products are Oeko Tex Certified and REACH certified. Also the company has ISO 9000 and 14000 certifications in its fold.


The company currently deals in almost 1000 shades and more than 500 product variants suited for specific needs. Adding on the world class infrastructure, definitely it’s a force to reckon with……..


With innovative manpower and flexible infrastructure the target is to have a new commercial product every week and the focus of R&D is imminent and continuous. This way it keeps in line with the changing trends and achieves better capacity utilisations by taking seasonal changes in its stride.
